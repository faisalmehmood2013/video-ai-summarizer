About Flask Video AI Summarizer

Welcome to our Flask-based Video AI Summarizer! This platform allows users to upload a video file or provide a YouTube URL and interact with an AI-powered assistant to extract key insights from the video.

How It Works

Our AI system processes video content and provides:

  • Summaries of the video using AI-based natural language processing (NLP).
  • Interactive chat to ask questions about the video and get AI-generated responses.
  • Relevant timestamps for easy navigation.
  • Stored chat history so users can revisit past discussions even after refreshing the page.

Technology Stack

We use the following technologies to power this AI-driven summarizer:

  • Flask: Backend framework managing web application and API requests.
  • Google Gemini AI: NLP model for summarization and question-answering.
  • Phi AI Agent: Structured chatbot integration for interactive conversations.
  • YouTubeTranscriptAPI: Fetches video transcripts when available.
  • yt-dlp: Downloads subtitles for videos without automatic transcripts.

Use Cases

This platform is beneficial in various fields, including:

  • Education: Students can quickly summarize lecture videos.
  • Corporate Training: Employees can review training materials efficiently.
  • News & Research: Journalists and researchers can extract insights from interviews and documentaries.
  • Content Creators: Analyze trends and key topics from videos.

Future Enhancements

We plan to introduce new features such as:

  • Multi-language support for video analysis.
  • Advanced sentiment analysis.
  • Custom AI fine-tuning for specific industries.
  • Voice-based interaction for hands-free AI engagement.

Thank you for using our AI Video Summarizer! We aim to make video consumption smarter and more interactive.
